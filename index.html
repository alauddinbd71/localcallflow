Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a website before we start?
Not necessarily. I can build a high-converting landing page as part of the system. For call-only ads, a website isn't required at all — the goal is to get people calling you, not browsing.
How long until I start getting more calls?
Google Ads can generate calls within days of launch. GBP improvements typically show results within 2–6 weeks. Local SEO is a longer-term investment — usually 2–4 months to see meaningful organic ranking movement. The system is designed to generate early quick wins while building long-term visibility.
What's the minimum ad budget I need?
For call-only campaigns in most local markets, a budget of $500–$1,500/month is a reasonable starting point, depending on your market size and competition. I'll give you a specific recommendation after seeing your market during the audit.
Do you only work with plumbing and HVAC businesses?
My system is built specifically for these two trades. That focus means everything — the keywords, ad copy, landing page messaging, and strategy — is refined for your type of business and customer. I don't dilute that by working across every industry.
What makes you different from a regular marketing agency?
Most marketers learn the tools and call themselves technical. I come from the opposite direction: nearly 7 years of real technical systems work — infrastructure, networking, complex troubleshooting — applied to local lead generation. I think in systems, not campaigns. That produces a different quality of result.
Do you offer any guarantees?
I don't make income or lead guarantees — no honest professional can ethically promise specific numbers without knowing your exact market. What I guarantee is the quality of the system, full transparency on results, and that you'll see exactly where every dollar and every call comes from. You'll always know what's working.
Can I hire you for just one service?
Yes. Each service is available individually. That said, the system produces significantly better results when the components work together — GBP + Ads + Landing Page is a much more powerful combination than any single piece alone.
